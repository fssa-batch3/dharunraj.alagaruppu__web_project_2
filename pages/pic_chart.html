Frequently Asked Questions
( FAQs )

1 Monthly average balance

Monthly Average Balance is the minimum amount of funds that account holders must maintain to avoid being penalized by the bank.

2Every day should maintain MAB

No. Since the daily closing balance is averaged out, the account holder may have more funds than MAB (Minimum average balance) on some days that can make up for the shortfall on some days.

3 MAB calculated by my bank

Banks use the formula: MAB = (total of end of the day closing balances) / (number of days in one month) to calculate the MAB of an account holder.
